Dosing & route guidance — *estimated effective dose is not a measured value

Consumed mg/day is the THC in the products entered (grams/day × %THC). Estimated effective mg/day applies a per-route bioavailability factor to approximate systemically absorbed THC. These factors are rough, highly variable estimates pending clinical validation (sources: Permanente Journal TPP/19.200; Upadhyay et al. J Cannabis Res 2023). A "+" on the effective total means one or more routes had no agreed factor.
